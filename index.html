Code is for people, not computers.

I firmly believe that the satisfaction of people must be the driving purpose behind engineering. From this belief I have built a career of serving all stakeholders in websites.

My work produces satisfaction for the site owners, end users, and my colleagues.

    • Developers

    Being a team player means making work that benefits the team.

    I don't strive to wow my colleagues with my code. I strive to empower them. That is why I favor whatever code solution enables the team to deliver its best work.

    I work to create clean, well-commented, and well-tested code. I am happy to use stable and battle-tested libraries and frameworks if it means that the next person can take my work and run with it.

    Example Work

    I constructed a modular markup and BEM infrastructure for a group of sites including hylinkexp.com. This allowed me to craft one site and reuse almost the entire codebase for other sites, such as hylinkhelix.com. The sites are backed by a multi-site installation of CraftCMS and coded using a gulp-backed optimization workflow.

    Clients

    Update a client's site and they profit for a day. Teach a client to update their site, and they profit for a lifetime.

    I do not consider my work launched until I have seen the site owner modify site content. I've cultivated expertise in product hand-offs because I firmly believe that empowerment is impossible without understanding.

    Example Work

    I built blla.org in WordPress with carefully-chosen plugins to provide an optimal content management experience. I facilitated extensive training and an iterative feedback proces to obtain full client buy-in.

    End Users

    A well-built website is like clean water: invisible, yet invigorating.

    To create an outstanding user experience is the driving force of my work. I consider both the site visitor and the site owner to be equally important users of the site. It should serve the needs of its users smoothly and effectively.

    Example Work

    I was primary developer on the Laravel-based eCommerce site gwcopiers.com. I leveraged VueJS and Model tagging to construct a clean and fast category filtering interface. On another Laravel-driven eCommerce app, I created a custom integration that allowed the site owner to seamlessly integrate sales into their their business using the NetSuite API.

    Management

    Reputation is built on bringing value to those you serve.

    My work represents the company that I work for. I take pride in delivering functional, high-visibility sites that convert.

    Example Work

    I built and launched a website for Sunkist, and then personally ensured that their team could use that site to complete product sales at a major annual convention immediately after launch. I have also been responsible for administering CentOS and Ubuntu-based servers running LAMP, hosting various high-uptime sites such as visitmarinadelrey.com
